About of Course

Master the art of styling websites with our comprehensive CSS course! Whether you're a beginner or looking to deepen your skills, this course will guide you through everything from the basics of CSS syntax to advanced techniques for creating responsive, visually stunning designs.

  • Understand selectors, properties, and the box model.
  • Dive into flexbox, grid, animations, and transitions.
  • Learn how to create websites that look great on all devices using media queries.
  • Build modern layouts and interactive designs to enhance your portfolio.
