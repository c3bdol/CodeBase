Introduction

Cyber security is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital threats, unauthorized access, and damage. It is an essential field in today's interconnected world, where technology plays a central role in personal, business, and governmental operations.

Threats

Cyber security threats refer to malicious activities aimed at accessing, disrupting, or damaging systems, networks, or data. These threats are a growing concern as technology continues to evolve, impacting individuals, businesses, and governments worldwide.
